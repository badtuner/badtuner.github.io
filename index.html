OPTIMIZED FOR DESKTOP
  1. The tiles turn pink when ON, and purple when OFF
  2. Select the tiles you want turned ON before pressing play.
  3. Each row represents a different looped instrument
  4. Each column represents a different phrase (4 bars). They will play one at the time from left to right
  5. Press play to start from the beginning. Any selections you make while your loop is playing will not be applied to the current loop playing.
  6. Any issues? Refresh your browser!
